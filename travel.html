AHMEDABAD

Ahmedabad, also known as Amdavad, is the largest city in Gujarat, India, situated on the banks of the Sabarmati River. Founded in 1411 by Sultan Ahmed Shah, it is a UNESCO World Heritage city renowned for its rich history, textile industry ("Manchester of India"), and as a major commercial and educational hub.

THAILAND

Thailand, officially the Kingdom of Thailand and formerly Siam, is a Southeast Asian nation renowned for its tropical beaches, opulent royal palaces, ancient ruins, and ornate temples. Bangkok is the capital and largest city, with a population of over 66 million. It is a constitutional monarchy.
